About Us

Function Junction is an event management company under the young and visionary leadership of Chairman and Managing Director Ms. Muskan Rekhani and Mr. Nikhil Agrawal. Started in the year 2019, the company has earned a name for itself in organizing both private and official events. We are a family of over 200 young hearts and creative minds. We are known for our quality work and organizing events with style, class, and ethnicity that touches every mind and soul.

From conceptual staging to the last exit we handle it all. The design and management of any event are handled by our brilliant team as it is a part of our own. We believe in delivering happiness with a captivating charm that you take back with yourself in the form of bedazzling photographs. Creating is what we believe in and once you work with us you won't need to knock on another door.
